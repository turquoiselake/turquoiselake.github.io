Marvel Omnibus Spider-man by Roger Stern

タイトル: Spider-man by Roger Stern Omnibus

Mighty Marvel presents Roger Stern"s celebrated 1980s run on SPECTACULAR SPIDER-MAN and AMAZING SPIDER-MAN in one sensational omnibus! Paired with John Romita Jr. and a host of other top art talents, Stern reinvigorated the friendly neighborhood Spider-Man - crafting definitive battles against the Juggernaut, Vulture and Kingpin; the fan-favorite tale of ""The Kid Who Collects Spider-Man""; the debut of Monica Rambeau (A.K.A. Captain Marvel) and the introduction of the Hobgoblin!

Collecting PETER PARKER, THE SPECTACULAR SPIDER-MAN (1976) #43-61 and #85 and AMAZING SPIDER-MAN (1963) #206, #224-252 and ANNUAL #16-17 - plus material from PETER PARKER, THE SPECTACULAR SPIDER-MAN ANNUAL #3; AMAZING SPIDER-MAN ANNUAL #15; WEB OF SPIDER-MAN ANNUAL #3 and WHAT IF? (1977) #34.
